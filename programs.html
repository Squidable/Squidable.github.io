Programs


Language: english and mandarin
Reading: circle time is the most effective way to develop language skills for children such as storytelling, rhyming songs
Math: learn to count numbers and recognize sequence patterns, Shape, and measures
Arts: painting, drawing, and colouring for fun, helping children develop their fine motor skills and creative thinking
Outdoor play: trying to walk, run, jump, hop, roll, throw, balance, lift at playgrounds every day to get stronger and developing children's curiosity about nature
Communication: expressing their needs,, being a good listener, taking turns in a conversation, expressing themselves clearly and with confidence
Food: Offering regularly scheduled lunch and snacks each day, serving food at designated eating table
